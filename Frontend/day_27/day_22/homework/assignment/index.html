Facebook Wiki

Facebook is one of the most Popular Social Media Made by Meta Inc. (Release: 2004). In Facebook, You can: See Friends, Play Facebook Games, Watch Videos, Change your Facebook Profile, Message others using Messenger and much more!
You can also create Groups and shop in Facebook Marketplace. You can communicate friends using Messenger, Viber and Whatsapp.

Facebook Content:

Facebook Posts have different type of Content (for ex: Educational, Photograph, Gaming and more..) Some of these contents are popular, and some are addicting. You can also react post, block people, comment on others posts, share on Messenger to your friends, save video to watch later and much more! there is also Reels. Reels allow you to create short posts, just like Youtube Shorts. There are many more social media apps (for ex: Snapchat, TikTok, Youtube, Twitter and more) but most of the people use Facebook. you can also connect to other games (for ex: PUBG) using Facebook and if one of your friends have it connected to Facebook, they will allow you to add you to game.

Facebook Marketplace:

Facebook Marketplace is almost like Ebay or Amazon. Facebook Marketplace allows you to buy things online. (for ex: Consoles, Gaming Headsets and more..) Its not that useful, since you can use Ebay or Amazon, but some people use it..
